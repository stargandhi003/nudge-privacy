Frequently Asked Questions

Where is my data stored?
All your data is stored locally on your device. Nudge does not use any external servers or cloud storage.
What happens if I delete the app?
All your data — trades, journal entries, daily plans, and settings — will be permanently deleted when you remove the app.
Does Nudge provide financial advice?
No. Nudge is a mindset and discipline tool. It does not provide financial advice, trading signals, or broker connections.
Does Nudge connect to my brokerage?
No. Nudge does not connect to any brokers or external services. The optional password vault stores credentials locally for your own reference only.
How do I reset my data?
You can reset your data by deleting and reinstalling the app. All locally stored data will be removed.
Is Nudge free?
Yes. Nudge is free to use with no in-app purchases or subscriptions.
