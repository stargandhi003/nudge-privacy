Nudge - Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 9, 2026

Overview

Nudge is a trading companion app that helps day traders plan, execute, and review their trades. Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains how we handle your data.

Data Collection

Nudge does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. All your data — including trade records, journal entries, daily plans, and settings — is stored locally on your device only.

No Analytics or Tracking

Nudge does not use any analytics, advertising, or tracking services. We do not track your behavior, location, or usage patterns.

No Third-Party Services

Nudge does not share data with any third parties. The app operates entirely offline and does not make network requests to external services.

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on your device using local databases and encrypted storage. If you delete the app, all your data will be permanently removed.

Broker Password Vault

Nudge includes an optional Broker Password Vault feature that allows you to store broker login credentials locally on your device. This data is stored using on-device encrypted storage and is never transmitted to any server or third party. You can enable or disable this feature at any time in Settings.

No Account Required

Nudge does not require you to create an account. There is no sign-up, login, or authentication process.

Children's Privacy

Nudge is not directed at children under 17. The app involves financial trading concepts and is intended for adult users.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update it within the app. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
